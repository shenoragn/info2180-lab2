The Higher the Monkey Climb.....

October 3,2021 by Shenor

'Di more di monkey climb, di more im backside expose', is a popular Jamaican prverb that outrightly reveals that people who desire fame or power should be prepared to have every aspect of their lives examined. Take note too that the good, the bad, the ugly and the indifferent will become noticeable by everyone. The popular notion is that the more an unsuitable person get promoted the more his/her inadequacies shows.I now see that this is a one sided view of the monkey and that view is the monkey's backside, therefore the monkey is now in a better position to (let's behave right here) on you. The other view is that the Monkey now has a better view of the world and can now appreciate it more. Anyways... to each his own.

'The nail that sticks up, WILL be hammered down.' If you are a carpenter building a house, this makes a lot of sense. Nails that stick out are dangerous, causing injury or damage to various things that can get snagged. We expect a carpenter to pound in that protruding nail. But this saying is referring to people.
